Shipping / FAQ

   ===========================
   == GENERAL INFORMATION ==
   ===========================

   – We ship from Germany to everywhere in Europe
   – We use business like envelopes with printed labels
   – We pack everything 2-3 times vacuum in safe packaging
   – We ship within 24 hours after we have received full payment in
   bitcoin
   – We do not store your information for longer than necessary
   – We send with tracking when your purchase is more as 300€
   – For small Domestic orders with tracking we use Deutsche Post Einwurf
   Einschreiben
   – For small Europe orders we use modern [112]rfid chips to track your
   order
   – Because of that we can track your order without signing
   – For bigger Domestic / Europe orders we use DHL tracking (signing
   required)
   – For security reasons we do not give tracking codes to customers
   – Always use PGP when sending your address
   – Write your address correctly
     __________________________________________________________________

   ==================
   == RESHIP POLICY ==
   ==================

   For tracked orders you are entitled to one 50% reship for every 5
   successful orders that you have made with us. This reship cannot exceed
   the average weight of the 5 successful orders. All other orders you
   have had with us on past market places count as well.

   ALWAYS CONTACT US if something does not arrive, we make refund
   decisions
   on a case by case basis and we are easy to get along with.

   We are here to help you out if something goes wrong. Thanks!!
