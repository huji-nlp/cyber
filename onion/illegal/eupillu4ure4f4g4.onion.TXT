   Yes, we ship worldwide, except US, Canada and Australia.

   shipments from lets say India. Should your shipment be seized you’ll be
   able to choose between a full refund or a free reshipment.

How long will it take to get my package?

   outside EU take 6-8 working days to be delivered. That said we’ve had
   orders inside EU wandering for 2 weeks before delivery and some US
   orders arriving in a few days, go figure.

What is Bitcoin?

   Bitcoin is a digital currency in which transactions can be done without
   the need for a third party, e.g. bank. Its a way for you to pay us
   without asking anyone’s permission.

   See our page [18]How to Buy Bitcoins >.

   You will get an email about completed payment. That usually happens 20
   minutes after you send Bitcoins.

Why do you always change Bitcoin address to which I have to pay?

   Every order gets a unique Bitcoin payment address. That’s done to
   increase security and anonymity.

Do you have other payment methods besides Bitcoin?

   No, we only support Bitcoin payments.

How to Buy Bitcoins

   [18]bittylicious2

   There are many places to get Bitcoins, but we recommend
   [19]Bittylicious.com, especially if you live in EU. Besides credit
   cards Bittylicious also supports SEPA payments, through which Bitcoins
   can be purchased via regular online banking in just 1-2 hours.

   The website is straightforward and doesn’t need much explanation.
   Bittylicous.com also provides a helpful [20]buying guide>.

   You will also need a wallet to store your Bitcoins. If you prefer your
   wallet to live in your computer we recommend [21]MultiBit. If you would
   like a cloud based wallet we heard good things about [22]BlockChain.
